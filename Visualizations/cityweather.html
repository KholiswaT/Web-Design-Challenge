How's The Weather Up There?

Analysis of the Occurrence of Weather Phenomenons in Cities at Increasing Latitudes

This project aims to investigate weather in cities at various latitudes. In this analysis, select weather phenomenons will be compared to cities at an increasing latitude. Specifically, the maximum temperature, wind speed, humidity and cloudiness will be analyzed by examining the plots of these phenomenons.
